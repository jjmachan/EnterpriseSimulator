# About Me

I'm Marcus Johnson, 45, and I teach AP US History at Lincoln High School in suburban Chicago.
I've been teaching for 20 years and I genuinely love it — my students say I tell the best stories.
I recently set up a home office because I started tutoring online on weekends for extra income.
My wife and I have three kids (16, 13, and 9), so the house is always loud and busy.
I bought a basic desk first to get started, but I'm upgrading to a standing desk because
my doctor told me I sit too much. I tend to write long emails — occupational hazard of
being a history teacher who loves a good narrative.
