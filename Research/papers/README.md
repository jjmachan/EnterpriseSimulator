# Research Papers

## CoreCraft Environment & Scalable RL

### The Hierarchy of Agentic Capabilities (2601.09032)
- **File:** `hierarchy_agentic_capabilities_2601.09032.pdf`
- **URL:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.09032
- **Authors:** Logan Ritchie, Sushant Mehta, Nick Heiner, Mason Yu, Edwin Chen
- **Summary:** Empirical evaluation of AI models on 150 workplace tasks in a realistic e-commerce RL environment. Identifies five critical capabilities: tool use, planning, adaptability, groundedness, and common-sense reasoning. Best models still fail ~40% of tasks.

### EnterpriseBench CoreCraft (2602.16179)
- **File:** `enterprisebench_corecraft_2602.16179.pdf`
- **URL:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.16179
- **Authors:** Sushant Mehta, Logan Ritchie, Suhaas Garre, Ian Niebres, Nick Heiner, Edwin Chen
- **Summary:** Introduces CoreCraft, an enterprise simulation with 2,500+ entities across 14 types and 23 tools. Shows training on high-fidelity RL environments produces generalizable capabilities. GLM 4.6 training improved pass rates from 25.37% to 36.76%. Transfer learning gains on BFCL, Tau2-Bench, and Tool Decathlon.

## Simulation & Generative Agents

### Joon Sung Park Dissertation
- **File:** `joon_sung_park_dissertation.pdf`
- **URL:** https://www.joonsungpark.com/papers/Joon_Sung_Park_Dissertation/
- **Summary:** PhD dissertation on generative agents and simulating human behavior with LLMs. Foundation for the generative agents architecture.

### Generative Agent Simulations of 1,000 People (2411.10109)
- **File:** `generative_agent_sims_1000_2411.10109.pdf`
- **URL:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2411.10109
- **Authors:** Joon Sung Park, Carolyn Q. Zou, Aaron Shaw, Benjamin Mako Hill, Carrie Cai, Meredith Ringel Morris, Robb Willer, Percy Liang, Michael S. Bernstein
- **Summary:** Created computational agents representing 1,052 real people using LLMs + qualitative interviews. Agents matched participant GSS responses with 85% accuracy relative to human self-replication. Reduced accuracy biases across racial/ideological groups.

### Generative Agents: Interactive Simulacra of Human Behavior (2304.03442)
- **File:** `generative_agents_simulacra_2304.03442.pdf`
- **URL:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2304.03442
- **Authors:** Joon Sung Park, Joseph C. O'Brien, Carrie J. Cai, Meredith Ringel Morris, Percy Liang, Michael S. Bernstein
- **Summary:** Introduces generative agents that simulate believable human behavior using LLMs + memory streams. 25 agents in a Sims-like sandbox autonomously organize social events, form relationships, and coordinate. Observation, planning, and reflection components each contribute to believable behavior.
